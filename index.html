About

Waqar is a Yorkshire (Sheffield) based Queer Sound Recordist and Sound Editor, with almost a decade of experience in film and video productions, ranging from documentaries, commercial, numerous short films, and indie features, having worked with clients such as BBC Bitesize, BFI, Lego, Warp Films, Google UK, and Netflix. Whilst he is based in Yorkshire, he covers projects all over the UK, having regularly travelled to London, Manchester, Birmingham, Cardiff, Glasgow, Edinburgh, and occasionally abroad.
